# Empyre - New Player Quickstart

Welcome to Empyre, a text-based empire building game! Build your kingdom, manage resources, wage war, and rise from Lord to Emperor.

## Your Goal

Rise through four ranks by building up your empire:
- **Lord** - Starting rank
- **Prince** - Requires 10+ markets, 5+ mills, 2+ foundries, 2+ shipyards, 3+ palaces, more nobles and serfs
- **King** - Requires even higher stats
- **Emperor** - The ultimate goal

## Starting Resources

When you begin, you receive:
- 5,000 acres of land
- ~2,000 serfs (population/workers)
- 3 nobles
- 40 soldiers
- 20,000 bushels of grain (food)
- 250,000 coins
- 1 palace, 1 market, 1 mill, 1 stable
- 2 foundries

## How Turns Work

Each turn flows through these steps automatically:

1. **Weather** - Affects your harvest
2. **Disasters** - Random events (plague, rats, earthquake, volcano, tidal wave)
3. **Harvest** - Grain production
4. **Town Activities** - Optional: train soldiers, change tax rate, buy land
5. **Combat** - Optional: attack other players
6. **Investments** - Buy/sell buildings and units
7. **Yearly Report** - See your income and expenses

## Invest in Buildings

Purchase buildings to increase revenue. Prices and income sources:

| Item | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Markets | $1,000 | Revenue based on serfs |
| Mills | $2,000 | Revenue based on grain harvest |
| Foundries | $7,000 | Revenue based on soldiers; needed for army size |
| Shipyards | $8,000 | Revenue based on land; build ships without limit |
| Stables | $10,000 | Holds up 50 horses each |
| Palaces | $20 | Adds defense; gives 1 noble each |
| Nobles | $25,000 | Needed for army (20 soldiers per noble) |
| Ships | $5,000 | Transport goods to/from your island |
| Soldiers | $20 | Your military |

## Important: Single-Key Input

**You don't need to press Enter!** When you see a menu prompt, just press the key for your choice:

```
Your command, Lord Bob? [IMNPTQY]:
```

Just press one key - the game responds immediately.

## Getting Help

- **?** - Shows available options at any menu
- **F1** - Same as ? (shows help)
- **Y** - View your current stats (works at most prompts)

## Key Strategies

- **Land:Serf Ratio** - Must stay above 7:1 (7 acres per serf). Go below and you lose your empire!

- **Feed Your People** - Serfs need 5 bushels of grain each. Underfeed to reduce population, overfeed to grow it. Underfeeding too much causes rebellions.

- **Feed Your Army** - Soldiers need 8 bushels each. Without enough grain, they go on strike.

- **Build Your Island** - Your island can produce valuable spices. Trade spices for exports, then sell exports at the dry dock for profit.

- **Watch for Disasters** - Random disasters strike each turn: plague kills people, rats eat grain, earthquakes damage palaces, volcanoes destroy markets/mills/foundries, tidal waves damage shipyards.

- **Diplomats Required** - You'll need at least 1 diplomat to advance past Lord rank.
